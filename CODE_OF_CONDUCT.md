# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Noeron to be a welcoming, harassment‑free project for everyone, regardless
of experience, background or identity. We expect everyone who participates — in
issues, pull requests, discussions and any project space — to help keep it that way.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, patient and constructive.
- Assume good faith; critique ideas and code, not people.
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started.
- Respect the project's purpose: lawful, authorised, open‑source investigation.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Using the project to facilitate illegal activity, intrusion, stalking or harassment.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove or reject contributions and comments that violate this
code, and may temporarily or permanently ban repeat or serious offenders.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behaviour privately to the maintainer (contact in the repo
profile / `git log`). Reports are handled confidentially and in good faith.

---

This code is intentionally short. For a fuller framework, see the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), on which community
expectations like these are commonly based.
